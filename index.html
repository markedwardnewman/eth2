Houston’s University

At the University of Houston, you’ll find a college experience that will transform your future to what you envision. Our nationally-ranked programs drive students to discover the multitude of academic resources that are available on-campus and online while our student-run organizations and athletics have something for everyone and connect students in authentic Coog culture. Community service is also a big part of the University of Houston’s commitment to engage students directly with local centers and nonprofits. We are Houston’s University.

UH’s Learning Abroad

The University of Houston’s Learning Abroad program offers undergraduate and graduate students unique, life changing, educational overseas experiences. It gives students the opportunity to participate in faculty-led, reciprocal exchange, and affiliated third party provider programs, as well as research collaboratives, internships, experiential and service learning, and volunteer activities.

Through University of Houston partnerships in Central and Latin America, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, Asia, and Australia — the options to study abroad are virtually unlimited. You will explore the world and immerse yourself in many different cultures.

A Diverse Student Body

One of the many qualities that makes the University of Houston special is its diverse culture. As an undergraduate or graduate student, you will meet classmates, faculty and staff from all walks of life. We rank #2 in the nation when it comes to diverse quality of life. The numbers don’t lie — 40,000 undergraduate and graduate students hail from over 137 countries.

Additionally, we boast more than 400+ student organizations, 17 intercollegiate sports teams and 9 residence communities that house more than 6,000 students. You don’t have to travel the world to learn about different cultures and meet new people — you just have to be a student at the University of Houston.

The Next Great Global City

Forbes named Houston as America’s next great global city and one of the country’s coolest places to live. As the fourth largest city in the United States, Houston is a great place for recent graduates as well. Houston is revered for job opportunities, affordable cost of living, millennial culture and cultural diversity.

Learn More

Discovery Green

Discovery Green is a beautiful, vibrant 12-acre urban park in the heart of Downtown Houston. The 11.78-acre park is located on across from the George R. Brown Convention Center and adjacent to the Toyota Center. Discovery Green includes a lake, venues for public performances, dog runs and recreational areas. The park was designed by Hargreaves Associates, the landscape architecture firm.

Learn More

Hobby Center

The Hobby Center for the Performing Arts sits in the middle of downtown Houston, in the heart of the Theater District. The two acoustically designed theaters, the breathtaking lobby and the renowed restaurant make the Hobby Center for Performing Arts the place to be to catch a spectacular performance.
